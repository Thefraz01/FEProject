The Marvelous Life of Jack Casali

We've all made wishes. Unfortunately for Jack Casali, his came true.

After selling his soul to the demon Devin McCabe, Jack realizes he's made a grave mistake. With his own redemption impossible, Jack sets out to save other people from making the same mistake by sabotaging Devin's deals with other "clients". Since their contract doesn't allow Devin to harm Jack, Devin must resort to other means to keep Jack out of his hair and arranges o have the reality show "The Marvelous Life" film a segment about Jack to keep him occupied.

Unfortunately for Jack, he is sidelined at the worst possible time. Just before the film crew arrives, staff and patrons at the bar and grill owned by Jack's friend, Jerry Brown, begin disappearing. When he investigates, Jack learns a murderous secret about he new club across the street, and that its mysterious owners are targeting the only real friend Jack has left.

Fractures


After their daughter is injured in a terrible accident, two parents race to the hospital, hoping for the best. With their daughter sedated and unresponsive, they are introduced to a new technology that allows them to communicate with her when they are forced to make life and death decisions.




Road Service

In this modern day campfire-story, Karen's car breaks down in the middle of nowhere while she is on the way to vacation with some friends. Finding herself at the mercy of strangers, Karen struggles to get back on the road, unable to escape the feeling that something isn't quite right.
